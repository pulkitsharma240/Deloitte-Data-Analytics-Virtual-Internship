# Task 2 — Gender Pay Equality Analysis Findings

## 🎯 Project Objective

Daikibo Industrials received multiple internal complaints regarding gender-based salary inequality.

The objective was to classify Equality Scores and identify factories and job roles requiring HR intervention.

---

## 📊 Dataset Used

The Excel dataset contained:

* Factory
* Job Role
* Equality Score

A new classification column was created:

## Equality Class

to categorise salary fairness.

---

## ⚖️ Classification Rules

### ✅ Fair

Equality Score between:

±10

### ⚠️ Unfair

Equality Score beyond fair range

### 🚨 Highly Discriminative

Very high inequality scores requiring urgent attention

---

## 🔍 Business Findings

The analysis identified:

* multiple job roles with unfair compensation patterns
* highly discriminative roles requiring immediate review
* factories where salary equality risks were significantly high

These findings indicate the need for stronger HR monitoring and compensation policy review.

---

## 📉 Business Impact

Gender inequality in compensation can result in:

* employee dissatisfaction
* lower retention rates
* compliance risks
* damaged employer reputation
* internal workplace conflict

---

## 💡 Final Recommendation

Deloitte should recommend:

## Immediate HR Compensation Review

for:

* highly discriminative job roles
* factories with repeated unfair salary patterns

This should include:

* compensation audits
* policy correction
* equal pay compliance strategy
* leadership accountability

This improves workplace fairness and long-term employee trust.

---

## 📌 Final Deliverable

The updated Excel file with the new:

## Equality Class

column was submitted successfully.
