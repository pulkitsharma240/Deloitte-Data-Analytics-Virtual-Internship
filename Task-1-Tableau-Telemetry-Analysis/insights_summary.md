# Task 1 — Factory Telemetry Analysis Insights

## 🎯 Project Objective

Daikibo Industrials collected telemetry data from 4 factories to identify machine downtime patterns and improve operational efficiency.

The main business questions were:

1. Which factory experienced the highest machine downtime?

2. Which machine types caused the highest breakdown frequency?

---

## 🏭 Factory Locations Analysed

The following factory locations were included:

* Daikibo Factory Meiyo (Tokyo, Japan)
* Daikibo Factory Seiko (Osaka, Japan)
* Daikibo Berlin (Germany)
* Daikibo Shenzhen (China)

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

### ✅ Highest Downtime Factory

Daikibo Factory Seiko

This factory showed the highest machine downtime compared to all other locations.

This indicates higher operational risk and a stronger need for maintenance optimisation.

---

### ✅ Most Frequently Breaking Machine

Laser Welder

The Laser Welder recorded the highest unhealthy machine status frequency inside Daikibo Factory Seiko.

This directly contributes to production delays and reduced factory efficiency.

---

## 📉 Business Impact

Frequent machine downtime leads to:

* production delays
* increased maintenance costs
* reduced manufacturing efficiency
* delayed customer deliveries
* lower operational profitability

---

## 💡 Final Recommendation

Deloitte should recommend:

## Priority Maintenance Strategy

for:

* Daikibo Factory Seiko
* Laser Welder machines

This includes:

* preventive maintenance scheduling
* machine health monitoring
* replacement planning
* predictive maintenance strategy

This will reduce downtime risk and improve factory productivity.
